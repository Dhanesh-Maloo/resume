About Me

Hello! I'm Dhanesh, a BTech Cyber Security student at GLS University in Ahmedabad, currently working toward my degree with an eye firmly set on a career defending real systems from real threats. I'm still early in the journey, but I've already built a genuine passion for vulnerability assessment and penetration testing (VAPT), secure coding, network security, and cryptography.

My mission is simple: to help organizations defend against cyber threats and build secure, resilient infrastructures. I spend my time outside the classroom in labs and CTF-style exercises — breaking things on purpose with tools like Burp Suite, OWASP ZAP, and Kali Linux so I understand how attackers think, then documenting the mitigations that would stop them.

I'm not claiming years of industry experience — I'm an aspiring security professional who learns by doing, reads a lot of write-ups, and is looking for an entry-level role or internship where I can apply what I've learned to real-world problems while continuing to grow.
