About Me

Hello! I'm Dhanesh, a BTech Computer Science (Cyber Security) student at GLS University in Ahmedabad, currently ranked 6th in my class with an SGPA of 8.95. Alongside my degree, I've worked hands-on as a Penetration Tester at Deltaware Solutions, a Software Developer at Infolabz, and an Ethical Hacker at Waytoweb Pvt. Ltd. — so I've gotten to sit on both sides of the fence: building applications and trying to break them.

My interests span two directions that feed into each other: application development, scripting, APIs, and workflow automation on one side; vulnerability assessment, penetration testing, and secure coding on the other. I spend my time outside the classroom in labs and CTF-style exercises — breaking things on purpose with tools like Burp Suite, OWASP ZAP, Nikto, and Kali Linux so I understand how attackers think, then documenting the mitigations that would stop them.

I'm always looking for the next hard problem to work on, whether that's automating a business process, hardening a web app, or shipping a full-stack project end to end.
